About

This is Anders, a writer and thinker interested in literature, history, philosophy, and the classical tradition.

What This Site Is

A space for longer-form writing—essays, reflections, and arguments about books, ideas, and the enduring questions that ancient and modern thinkers have grappled with.

The writing here tends toward: - Literature: How we read, what we read, and why certain books endure - History: Not just what happened, but how we understand what happened - Philosophy: Ideas about knowledge, ethics, meaning, and how to live - Classical Studies: Greek and Roman thought, literature, and their influence on Western intellectual history

Why This Exists

Most online writing is optimized for speed—hot takes, quick threads, disposable content. This site is deliberately slower. The essays here are meant to be read carefully, perhaps more than once.

The goal isn't to persuade you of a particular worldview, but to think through difficult questions with clarity and honesty. Sometimes that means defending unpopular positions. Sometimes it means changing my mind.
